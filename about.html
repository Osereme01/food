About Us

OUR STORY

A celebration of Nigerian cultural diversity and natural environment.  Greme Foods showcases the best contemporary cuisine that demonstrates the highly skilled techniques and relentless drive for perfection that has become Chef Gracious Uwagbale's trademark.


Five time winner Best Chef in Lagos Nigeria.

Our Restaurant(Greme Foods Restaurant), is glamorous and elegant, yet relaxed and welcoming with warm and attentive services, we offer the best. Greme Foods Restaurant is located in Festac Lagos Nigeria.

We are open for all kinds of Local and Inter-continental dishes, with inbar dining menus and it's own private room, Greme Foods deliver an array of experiences. We believe in Quality, all around. Quality food can't be made without quality initiative.

Nobody cherishes food more than Greme Foods.
